Why collaboration matters

Break the Stigma

Addiction thrives in silence. When community organisations, faith leaders, and services stand together — it sends a powerful message that help is available and asking for support is not a sign of weakness.

Start the Conversation

Many families don't know where to turn. Partners help us reach people earlier — through trusted voices, familiar spaces, and languages that feel like home.

Shape Better Services

We feed our community findings directly to Public Health and commissioned services. Your involvement helps ensure those findings reflect the real experiences of the communities you serve.

What does partnership look like?

01
Join a Listening Session

Bring your community's voice into our focus groups. Help us capture experiences that would otherwise go unheard.

02
Share & Signpost

Distribute our bilingual resources, display our posters, and refer families who need support.

03
Co-produce With Us

Work alongside us to develop culturally informed resources, training, or campaigns that reflect your community's needs.
