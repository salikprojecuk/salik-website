Rochdale Science Extravaganza 2026

Sunday 28 June 2026 10:30am – 4:00pm Rochdale Exchange Shopping Centre

The Salik Project UK will have a stall at the Rochdale Science Extravaganza 2026. Come and find us at Rochdale Exchange Shopping Centre for information, resources and a conversation about addiction awareness in our communities.

We will also be announcing the winner of our Addiction Awareness Poster Competition — designed to raise awareness around nitrous oxide and cannabis misuse among young people.
