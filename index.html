"To Serve

The Salik Project UK is a Community Interest Company based in Rochdale, Greater Manchester. We work with South Asian individuals affected by addiction — and the families who love them.

We believe recovery isn't a solo journey. It requires informed families, trusted community support, and early, sustained engagement from services that understand culture, faith and language.

Our work spans community research, focus groups, bilingual resources in English, Urdu and Bengali, and partnerships with NHS and commissioned drug and alcohol services.
