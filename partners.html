Rochdale Communities Fund — Drug & Alcohol Diversity Programme 2026–2027

The Salik Project UK is grateful to Action Together for funding this project through the Rochdale Communities Fund, Drug and Alcohol Diversity Programme 2026–2027. Their investment makes our community research, outreach and education work possible.

Supporting South Asian individuals in recovery and the families who walk alongside them. Reducing stigma through education, culturally informed signposting and grassroots research.
