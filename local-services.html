Local Recovery Services.

Free, confidential support available in Rochdale and Oldham.

TP
Turning Point ROAR
Rochdale Oldham Adults

The main NHS-commissioned drug and alcohol service for Rochdale and Oldham. Medically assisted recovery, detox, rehab, psychosocial support, employment help and naloxone kits. Self-refer online or by phone.

0300 555 0234
Visit Website
EB
Early Break
Rochdale Oldham Young People Families

Free confidential support for young people under 21 and families affected by parental substance use. Counselling, drug and alcohol treatment, emotional wellbeing and advocacy.

0161 723 3880 info@earlybreak.co.uk
Visit Website
HN
High Level Northern Trust
Rochdale Adults

A safe space for anyone in recovery from any form of addictive behaviour. Peer support, holistic recovery programme and empowerment. Also supports families and friends.

01706 860247 highlevel@hlnt.org.uk
Visit Website
RR
Recovery Republic
Rochdale Adults

A wellbeing centre in Heywood offering counselling, befriending, drug and alcohol support, peer-led activities and therapeutic groups. Open to everyone in the community.

01706 622722 info@recoveryrepublic.co.uk
Visit Website
RC
Rochdale Recovery Communities
Rochdale Adults

A coalition of local organisations dedicated to ending addiction stigma, amplifying lived experience voices and building a visible recovery community across Rochdale.

recoveryrochdale@gmail.com
Visit Website
AA
Alcoholics Anonymous
Rochdale Oldham Adults

Free peer support groups for anyone wanting to stop drinking. The 12-step programme offers regular meetings and telephone support across Rochdale and Greater Manchester.

0800 9177 650
Visit Website
NA
Narcotics Anonymous
Rochdale Oldham Adults

Free peer support and fellowship for people recovering from drug addiction. Regular meetings across Greater Manchester with a 12-step approach.

0300 999 1212
Visit Website
