Addiction Awareness Poster Competition — Rochdale

By Mustafa Hameed  ·  20 April 2026

As part of our outreach engagement and addiction awareness with younger people we have partnered with the wonderful Rochdale Science Initiative for a poster competition.

There are three prizes to be won:

  • First Prize: £50.00 Amazon Voucher
  • Two runner-up prizes of £25.00 Amazon vouchers each

To receive guidelines for the competition, please get in touch: contact@salikprojectuk.org
